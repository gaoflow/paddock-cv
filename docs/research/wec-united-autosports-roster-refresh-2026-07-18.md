This pass corrected the current-team boundary and expanded United Autosports
beyond its two named car engineers. Current official team records were joined
to 2026 Le Mans reporting. Historical roles remain career evidence but are not
displayed as current team assignments.

## Avatar decisions

The official United page supplies 1000 x 1200 static portraits for Brown,
Gregory, Pucci, Hodgson, Foster and Dean. All six files were downloaded,
decoded and visually inspected in a contact sheet. They are clear,
single-person portraits in team clothing.

The James Foster card references an image whose filename contains
`Rodney-Foster`. The official card itself binds the image to James Foster and
his role; the filename discrepancy is retained in the structured notes for
future review.

No safe static portrait was found for Gary Robertshaw. Search results provided
current role evidence, group or garage scenes, and unrelated namesakes, but no
unambiguous face image. He remains published with initials rather than an
uncertain avatar.
